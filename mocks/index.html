ECG learning-mode mocks

Seven sample interactions across the four modes. Each weaves the AI tutor into the loop. Stylized strips, not real signals.

Guided tutorials

A · Guided discovery
Bundle branch blocks. Measure the QRS with a caliper, summon an LBBB to compare, flow adapts to your level (New / Building / Strong).
 B · Animated concept
Hyperkalemia. Drag serum K⁺ and watch the ECG morph (peaked T → wide QRS → sine); the tutor narrates each stage and quizzes you.

Rapid recognition

1 · Sick / not sick
10-second clock, "act now / work up / not sick", terse attending verdict, over-triage penalty, running accuracy + speed.
 2 · Name the rhythm
Type the rhythm in your own words; the tutor grades the call and tightens your wording (e.g. "irregular" → name it: AFib).

Case-based

1 · Read to a disposition
Inferior STEMI. You lead the systematic sweep; the tutor connects ECG → artery → action (cath lab, RV caution).
 2 · Serial comparison + present
Today vs the prior ECG — spot the change (Wellens), then present it to the AI attending, who probes your plan.

Concept drill

Massed practice — AV conduction
Back-to-back strips: normal vs 1° vs 3° block. Discriminator coaching + a mastery bar that fills as you streak.
